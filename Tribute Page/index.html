Charles Leclerc

Charles Leclerc driving the Ferrari SF90 at Circuit de Barcelona

Here's Charles' career progression:

  • 2005 - Begun his carting career
  • 2010 - Moved up to the KF3 class
  • 2012 - Moved again up to KF2
  • 2014 - Graduated to single-seaters
  • 2015 - Started racing in the FIA Formula 3 European Championship
  • 2016 - Accepted to the Ferrari Driver Academy and started testing in F1
  • 2017 - Confirmed to be graduating to the Formula 2 series
  • 2018 - Made his racing debut in Formula One, signing with the Sauber F1 Team as regular driver
  • 2019 - Replaced Kimi Räikkönen at Scuderia Ferrari
Leclerc is due to drive for Ferrari until the end of 2024!
